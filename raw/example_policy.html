Warranty Policy

All physical products include a limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

Coverage Period

The standard warranty lasts 12 months from the delivery date. Refurbished items are covered for 90 days.

What Is Not Covered

  • Accidental damage & water damage
  • Normal wear & tear
  • Products with a removed or altered serial number

How to File a Claim

Email support@fever.example with your order number and a photo of the defect. Claims are reviewed within 3 business days.
